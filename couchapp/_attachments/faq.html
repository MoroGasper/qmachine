Frequently Asked Questions
What browser should I use?
We recommend Google Chrome highly, but Safari, Opera, and Firefox should also work. Internet Explorer lacks many advanced features that are present in modern browsers, including some required by Quanah. We are investigating Google's new Chrome Frame plugin to see if it patches IE suitably our needs, but we do not currently support Internet Explorer.
